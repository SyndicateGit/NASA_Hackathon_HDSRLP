NASA Space Facts

1.	Out of the eight planets, Mercury and Venus are the only ones with no moon.

2.	Saturn is the only planet in our solar system that is less dense than water. 

3.	Neptune’s winds can reach speeds of up to 2,575 kph. 

4.	NASA’s Wilkinson Microwave Anisotropy Probe (WMAP) determined the universe to be 13.77 billion years old.
